## Chapter 1: What is Risk? 什么是风险？

### 1.1 Definitions

**EN:** Risk is the possibility of loss or adverse outcome resulting from uncertainty. In finance and business, risk is not inherently bad — it is the source of return. The goal of risk management is not to eliminate risk, but to understand it, measure it, and ensure that the returns justify the risks taken.

**ZH:** 风险是由于不确定性导致损失或不利结果的可能性。在金融和商业中，风险本身并非坏事——它是回报的来源。风险管理的目标不是消除风险，而是理解它、衡量它，并确保回报值得所承担的风险。

### 1.2 Key Concepts 核心概念

| English | 中文 | Definition |
|---------|------|------------|
| Risk appetite | 风险偏好 | The amount and type of risk an organization is willing to accept in pursuit of its objectives |
| Risk tolerance | 风险容忍度 | The specific maximum risk that can be taken for each category |
| Risk capacity | 风险承受能力 | The absolute maximum loss an entity can absorb without failing |
| Risk exposure | 风险敞口 | The amount of potential financial loss from a specific risk |
| Risk profile | 风险画像 | A comprehensive view of all risks faced by an entity |
| Risk register | 风险登记册 | A documented list of identified risks, their assessments, and mitigation plans |
| Residual risk | 剩余风险 | The risk remaining after controls have been applied |
| Inherent risk | 固有风险 | The raw risk before any controls are applied |

### 1.3 The Risk Management Cycle 风险管理循环

```
IDENTIFY → ASSESS → MITIGATE → MONITOR → REPORT → REVIEW → IDENTIFY
  识别  →  评估  →  缓释   →  监控   →  报告  →  审查   →  识别
```

**EN:** Risk management is a continuous cycle, not a one-time activity. Each iteration improves the organization's resilience.

**ZH:** 风险管理是一个持续循环，不是一次性活动。每次迭代都提升组织的抗风险能力。

### 1.4 Risk Categories 风险分类

| English | 中文 | Examples |
|---------|------|----------|
| Market risk | 市场风险 | Price volatility, interest rate changes, FX fluctuations |
| Credit risk | 信用风险 | Counterparty default, settlement failure |
| Operational risk | 操作风险 | Human error, system failure, fraud, process failure |
| Liquidity risk | 流动性风险 | Inability to meet obligations, market depth insufficient |
| Legal & regulatory risk | 法律合规风险 | Regulatory fines, license revocation, lawsuits |
| Strategic risk | 战略风险 | Business model disruption, competitive threats |
| Reputational risk | 声誉风险 | Public scandal, loss of trust, media exposure |
| Systemic risk | 系统性风险 | Contagion, domino effects across the financial system |

## Chapter 2: The Three Lines of Defense 三道防线

### 2.1 The Model

**EN:** The Three Lines of Defense model is the global standard for organizing risk governance:

1. **First Line — Operational Management**: The business units that own and manage risk day-to-day. They are responsible for identifying, assessing, and controlling risks in their operations. "Risk ownership."

2. **Second Line — Risk & Compliance Functions**: Independent functions that set risk policies, define risk appetite, monitor risk levels, and ensure compliance with regulations. They provide oversight and challenge. "Risk oversight."

3. **Third Line — Internal Audit**: Fully independent function that provides assurance to the board and senior management on the effectiveness of risk management and internal controls. "Independent assurance."

**ZH:** 三道防线模型是全球风险治理的标准：

1. 第一道防线——运营管理层：日常管理和控制风险的业务部门。负责识别、评估和控制其运营中的风险。"风险所有权"。
2. 第二道防线——风险与合规职能部门：独立制定风险政策、定义风险偏好、监控风险水平并确保合规。提供监督和质疑。"风险监督"。
3. 第三道防线——内部审计：完全独立的部门，向董事会和高级管理层保证风险管理和内部控制的有效性。"独立保证"。

### 2.2 Why It Matters

**EN:** Without clear segregation, conflicts of interest arise. A trading desk that generates profit should not be the same team that monitors its risk-taking. This principle applies equally to crypto — a DeFi protocol should not have the same entity writing code and auditing it.

**ZH:** 没有清晰的职责分离，就会产生利益冲突。产生利润的交易台不应同时是监控其风险承担行为的团队。这一原则同样适用于加密世界——DeFi 协议不应由同一实体编写代码和审计代码。

## Chapter 3: Risk Measurement 风险计量

### 3.1 Value at Risk (VaR) 风险价值

**EN:** VaR estimates the maximum potential loss over a given time horizon at a given confidence level.

> "We are 99% confident that we will not lose more than $X over the next N days."

**Common parameters:**
- Confidence level: 95% or 99%
- Time horizon: 1 day (trading) or 10 days (regulatory capital)
- Calculation methods: Historical simulation, Variance-Covariance, Monte Carlo

**Limitations:**
- VaR does not capture tail risk beyond the confidence level
- Assumes normal market conditions
- Does not indicate the magnitude of losses beyond the VaR threshold

**ZH:** VaR 估计在给定时间范围和置信水平下的最大潜在损失。常用参数：95%/99%置信度，1天/10天时间范围。局限性：不捕捉尾部风险、假设正常市场条件、不指示超出阈值后的损失规模。

### 3.2 Expected Shortfall (ES) / CVaR 预期亏损

**EN:** Expected Shortfall (also called Conditional VaR or CVaR) measures the average loss in the worst cases beyond VaR. It addresses VaR's main weakness by capturing tail risk.

> "If we exceed our VaR, how bad will it be on average?"

**ZH:** 预期亏损（也叫条件 VaR 或 CVaR）衡量超出 VaR 的最坏情况下的平均损失。它通过捕捉尾部风险来解决 VaR 的主要弱点。

### 3.3 Stress Testing 压力测试

**EN:** Stress testing simulates extreme but plausible scenarios to assess an organization's resilience.

**Scenario types:**
- Historical scenarios (e.g., 2008 financial crisis, 2020 COVID crash)
- Hypothetical scenarios (e.g., 50% market drop, simultaneous defaults)
- Reverse stress testing (what scenario would break the organization?)

**Key questions:**
1. What is the worst that could happen?
2. Can we survive it?
3. How much capital would we need?
4. What is our contingency plan?

**ZH:** 压力测试模拟极端但合理的情景来评估组织的韧性。情景类型包括历史情景、假设情景和反向压力测试。关键问题：最坏会怎样？我们能活下来吗？需要多少资本？应急预案是什么？

### 3.4 Key Risk Indicators (KRIs) 关键风险指标

| English | 中文 | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Value at Risk (VaR) | 风险价值 | Daily VaR at 99% confidence |
| Concentration ratio | 集中度比率 | Top 5 positions / total portfolio |
| Leverage ratio | 杠杆比率 | Total exposure / equity |
| Liquidity coverage ratio | 流动性覆盖率 | High-quality liquid assets / net cash outflows |
| Error rate | 差错率 | Failed transactions / total transactions |
| Incident frequency | 事件频率 | Risk incidents per month |
| Audit findings | 审计发现 | Open high-risk findings |
| Staff turnover | 员工流动率 | Monthly attrition rate |

## Chapter 4: Internal Control Frameworks 内部控制框架

### 4.1 COSO Framework

**EN:** The COSO (Committee of Sponsoring Organizations) framework is the most widely adopted internal control standard globally. It defines five components:

1. **Control Environment**: The tone at the top — ethics, integrity, board oversight, organizational structure
2. **Risk Assessment**: Identifying and analyzing risks to achieving objectives
3. **Control Activities**: Policies and procedures that ensure management directives are carried out
4. **Information & Communication**: Relevant information identified, captured, and communicated
5. **Monitoring Activities**: Ongoing evaluations of the effectiveness of internal controls

**ZH:** COSO 框架是全球最广泛采用的内部控制标准。五个组成部分：控制环境、风险评估、控制活动、信息与沟通、监控活动。

### 4.2 Key Control Concepts

| English | 中文 | Definition |
|---------|------|------------|
| Segregation of duties | 职责分离 | No single person should control all aspects of a transaction |
| Authorization limits | 授权限额 | Transactions above certain thresholds require higher-level approval |
| Reconciliation | 对账 | Comparing two sets of records to ensure they match |
| Physical controls | 实物控制 | Locks, cameras, access cards — physical security measures |
| IT general controls | IT通用控制 | Access management, change management, backup procedures |
| Application controls | 应用控制 | Input validation, processing checks, output verification |
| Preventive controls | 预防性控制 | Controls that prevent errors before they occur |
| Detective controls | 检测性控制 | Controls that identify errors after they occur |
| Corrective controls | 纠正性控制 | Controls that fix errors after detection |

## Chapter 5: Market Risk Management 市场风险管理

### 5.1 Definition

**EN:** Market risk is the risk of losses in on- and off-balance sheet positions arising from movements in market prices. This includes:

- **Equity risk**: Stock price fluctuations
- **Interest rate risk**: Changes in interest rates
- **Foreign exchange risk**: Currency rate movements
- **Commodity risk**: Raw material price changes

**ZH:** 市场风险是由于市场价格变动导致表内和表外头寸损失的风险。包括股权风险、利率风险、汇率风险和大宗商品风险。

### 5.2 Hedging Strategies 套保策略

| English | 中文 | Description |
|---------|------|-------------|
| Natural hedge | 自然对冲 | Offsetting exposure through business operations |
| Forward contract | 远期合约 | Agreement to buy/sell at a future date at a fixed price |
| Futures | 期货 | Standardized exchange-traded forward contracts |
| Options | 期权 | Right but not obligation to buy/sell at a strike price |
| Swaps | 互换 | Exchange of cash flows between two parties |
| Cross-hedging | 交叉套保 | Hedging one asset with a correlated but different asset |

### 5.3 The Greek Letters 希腊字母

| Greek | 中文 | What it measures |
|-------|------|-----------------|
| Delta (Δ) | 德尔塔 | Sensitivity to underlying price changes |
| Gamma (Γ) | 伽马 | Rate of change of Delta |
| Theta (Θ) | 西塔 | Time decay of option value |
| Vega (ν) | 维加 | Sensitivity to volatility changes |
| Rho (ρ) | 柔 | Sensitivity to interest rate changes |

### 5.4 Commodity Hedging: The LME Nickel Case 大宗商品套保：伦镍事件

**EN:** The 2022 LME nickel crisis is one of the most significant commodity risk events in history. Key facts:

- Nickel price surged from ~$30,000 to >$100,000/ton in 2 days
- Tsingshan Holding Group held a massive short position (~200,000 tons)
- Margin calls exceeded $8 billion
- LME suspended trading and cancelled transactions — unprecedented action

**Root causes:**
1. Position concentration — too large relative to market liquidity
2. Delivery mismatch — physical nickel was not LME-deliverable grade
3. Inadequate margin buffers for extreme scenarios
4. OTC positions invisible to exchange risk systems
5. Geopolitical shock — Russia-Ukraine war disrupted nickel supply

**Lessons:**
- Always stress-test for "impossible" scenarios
- Ensure deliverable supply matches derivative positions
- OTC positions must be aggregated with exchange positions
- Counterparty credit risk in OTC markets is significant
- Liquidity risk can become existential in hours, not days

**ZH:** 2022年伦镍危机是历史上最重要的大宗商品风险事件之一。镍价两日内从约3万飙升至超10万美元/吨。青山控股持有约20万吨空头头寸，追保超过80亿美元。LME暂停交易并取消交易——前所未有。

根因：仓位集中度过高、交割品不匹配、极端情景保证金不足、OTC头寸不可见、俄乌战争供给冲击。

教训：永远为"不可能"的情景做压力测试、确保可交割供应匹配衍生品头寸、OTC头寸必须与交易所头寸合并计算、对手方信用风险在OTC市场很重大、流动性风险可能在几小时内（而非几天内）成为生死问题。

## Chapter 6: Credit Risk Management 信用风险管理

### 6.1 Definition

**EN:** Credit risk is the risk that a borrower or counterparty will fail to meet its obligations in accordance with agreed terms. In trading, this includes settlement risk (Herstatt risk) — the risk that one party pays but the counterparty fails to deliver.

**ZH:** 信用风险是借款人或对手方未能按约定条款履行义务的风险。在交易中，还包括结算风险——一方已付款但对手方未交割的风险。

### 6.2 The Five Cs of Credit 信用5C原则

| C | English | 中文 | Meaning |
|---|---------|------|---------|
| Character | 品格 | 借款人品格 | Reputation and track record |
| Capacity | 能力 | 还款能力 | Cash flow and ability to repay |
| Capital | 资本 | 资本实力 | Financial resources and net worth |
| Collateral | 担保 | 抵押担保 | Assets pledged as security |
| Conditions | 条件 | 外部条件 | Economic and industry conditions |

### 6.3 Credit Risk Metrics 信用风险指标

| English | 中文 | Formula/Meaning |
|---------|------|----------------|
| Probability of Default (PD) | 违约概率 | Likelihood of default within a timeframe |
| Loss Given Default (LGD) | 违约损失率 | Percentage of exposure lost if default occurs |
| Exposure at Default (EAD) | 违约风险敞口 | Total exposure at the time of default |
| Expected Loss (EL) | 预期损失 | EL = PD × LGD × EAD |
| Credit VaR | 信用风险价值 | Unexpected loss at a confidence level |
| Credit rating | 信用评级 | AAA to D (S&P/Fitch) or Aaa to C (Moody's) |

### 6.4 Credit Risk Mitigation 信用风险缓释

| Method | 中文 | How it works |
|--------|------|-------------|
| Collateral | 抵押 | Assets pledged to secure the obligation |
| Netting | 净额结算 | Offsetting mutual obligations |
| Credit derivatives | 信用衍生品 | CDS — transfer credit risk to a third party |
| Guarantees | 担保 | Third party agrees to pay if borrower defaults |
| Covenants | 契约条款 | Contractual restrictions on borrower behavior |
| Diversification | 分散化 | Spreading exposure across multiple counterparties |

## Chapter 7: Operational Risk Management 操作风险管理

### 7.1 Definition

**EN:** Operational risk is the risk of loss resulting from inadequate or failed internal processes, people, systems, or from external events. This includes legal risk but excludes strategic and reputational risk (under Basel II/III definitions).

**ZH:** 操作风险是由于不完善或失败的内部流程、人员、系统或外部事件导致损失的风险。包括法律风险，但不包括战略和声誉风险（根据巴塞尔II/III定义）。

### 7.2 Basel Seven Event Types 巴塞尔七类事件

| Type | 中文 | Examples |
|------|------|----------|
| Internal fraud | 内部欺诈 | Employee theft, insider trading, unauthorized trading |
| External fraud | 外部欺诈 | Hacking, robbery, check fraud, cyber attacks |
| Employment practices | 雇佣实践 | Discrimination, workplace safety, labor disputes |
| Clients, products & business practices | 客户/产品/业务实践 | Mis-selling, fiduciary breaches, money laundering |
| Damage to physical assets | 实物资产损害 | Natural disasters, terrorism, vandalism |
| Business disruption & system failures | 业务中断与系统故障 | IT outages, telecom failures, utility disruptions |
| Execution, delivery & process management | 执行/交付/流程管理 | Data entry errors, failed settlements, documentation errors |

### 7.3 Operational Risk Tools 操作风险工具

| Tool | 中文 | Purpose |
|------|------|---------|
| RCSA (Risk & Control Self-Assessment) | 风险与控制自评 | Business units assess their own risks and controls |
| KRI (Key Risk Indicators) | 关键风险指标 | Metrics that signal increasing risk levels |
| Loss data collection | 损失数据收集 | Recording and analyzing historical loss events |
| Scenario analysis | 情景分析 | Expert judgment on extreme but plausible events |
| Business continuity planning (BCP) | 业务连续性计划 | Plans to maintain operations during disruptions |
| Disaster recovery (DR) | 灾难恢复 | IT systems recovery after catastrophic failure |

### 8.2 KYC / AML Framework

**EN:** The KYC/AML framework has four key pillars:

1. **Customer Identification Program (CIP)**: Verify customer identity using reliable, independent source documents
2. **Customer Due Diligence (CDD)**: Understand the customer's business, ownership structure, and expected transaction patterns
3. **Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)**: Additional scrutiny for high-risk customers (PEPs, high-risk jurisdictions, complex structures)
4. **Ongoing Monitoring**: Continuous transaction monitoring against expected behavior, sanctions screening, and periodic reviews

**ZH:** KYC/AML 框架四大支柱：客户身份识别、客户尽职调查、强化尽职调查（针对高风险客户）、持续监控（交易监控、制裁筛查、定期审查）。

### 8.3 Red Flags for Money Laundering 洗钱红旗信号

| English | 中文 |

| Rapid movement of funds between multiple accounts with no business purpose | 资金在多个账户间快速转移，无商业目的 |
| Transactions involving high-risk jurisdictions (FATF grey/black list countries) | 涉及高风险司法管辖区的交易 |

| Shell companies with no physical presence or real business activity | 无实体存在或真实业务活动的空壳公司 |
| Use of mixers / tumblers for cryptocurrency transactions | 使用混币器进行加密货币交易 |
| Inconsistent transaction patterns relative to customer profile | 交易模式与客户画像不一致 |

## Chapter 10: Smart Contract Risk 智能合约风险

### 10.1 Common Vulnerability Patterns 常见漏洞模式

**Reentrancy 重入攻击**
> **EN:** A contract calls an external contract, which then calls back into the original contract before the first invocation completes, potentially draining funds. The classic example is the 2016 DAO hack ($60M).
> **ZH:** 合约调用外部合约，外部合约在第一次调用完成前回调原合约，可能盗取资金。经典案例：2016年DAO黑客事件（6000万美元）。
>
> **Mitigation**: Use Checks-Effects-Interactions pattern, ReentrancyGuard (OpenZeppelin)

**Integer Overflow/Underflow 整数溢出**
> **EN:** An arithmetic operation exceeds the maximum or minimum value a variable can hold, causing unexpected behavior. Solidity 0.8+ has built-in overflow checks.
> **ZH:** 算术运算超过变量能承载的最大或最小值，导致意外行为。Solidity 0.8+ 内置溢出检查。

**Access Control 访问控制缺陷**
> **EN:** Functions that should be restricted are left unprotected, allowing anyone to call them. Common mistakes include missing `onlyOwner` modifiers, incorrectly implemented role checks, or using `tx.origin` for authorization.
> **ZH:** 本应受限的函数未加保护，任何人都能调用。常见错误包括缺少 onlyOwner 修饰符、角色检查实现错误、使用 tx.origin 做授权。

**Flash Loan Attack 闪电贷攻击**
> **EN:** An attacker borrows a large amount of assets in a single transaction (no collateral required for flash loans), manipulates prices, exploits a protocol, and repays the loan — all in one atomic transaction.
> **ZH:** 攻击者在单笔交易中借入大量资产（闪电贷无需抵押），操纵价格、利用协议漏洞、偿还贷款——全在一笔原子交易中完成。

**Front-running 抢先交易**
> **EN:** A miner, validator, or bot observes a pending transaction in the mempool and submits their own transaction with a higher gas price to execute first, profiting from the victim's trade.
> **ZH:** 矿工/验证者/机器人观察到内存池中的待处理交易，以更高 Gas 价提交自己的交易抢先执行，从受害者的交易中获利。

**Oracle Manipulation 预言机操纵**
> **EN:** An attacker manipulates the price feed that a smart contract relies on, usually by executing large trades on the source DEX to temporarily skew the price, then exploiting the manipulated price in the target protocol.
> **ZH:** 攻击者操纵智能合约依赖的价格源，通常通过在源 DEX 执行大额交易暂时扭曲价格，然后在目标协议中利用被操纵的价格。

### 10.2 Audit Checklist 审计清单

```
Pre-Deployment Audit Checklist:
□ Reentrancy protection (CEI pattern or ReentrancyGuard)
□ Integer overflow/underflow (Solidity 0.8+ or SafeMath)
□ Access control on all sensitive functions
□ Input validation on all external functions
□ Oracle data source reliability (use TWAP, not spot price)
□ Flash loan attack vectors assessed
□ Front-running risks identified and mitigated
□ Randomness source (never use on-chain data for randomness)
□ Governance parameters reviewed (timelock, quorum, threshold)
□ Upgrade mechanism security (proxy pattern, storage gaps)
□ Emergency pause/stop mechanism
□ Event emissions for all state changes
□ Gas optimization review
□ External contract interaction safety
□ Withdrawal patterns (pull over push)
```

## Chapter 11: Exchange & Trading Risk 交易所与交易风险

### 11.1 Market Manipulation Typology 市场操纵类型

**Wash Trading 对敲交易**
> **EN:** A trader simultaneously buys and sells the same asset to create artificial trading volume. In crypto, this often involves connected wallets trading back and forth. On DEXes, it's cheap to do because of low fees.
> **Detection**: Look for circular fund flows, same-entity wallet clusters, high volume with minimal price impact.
> **ZH:** 交易者同时买卖相同资产制造虚假交易量。在加密世界，通常涉及关联钱包之间的来回交易。在DEX上成本低廉。检测方法：查找循环资金流、同一实体钱包集群、高交易量但价格影响极小。

**Spoofing 幌骗**
> **EN:** Placing large orders with the intent to cancel them before execution, creating a false impression of supply or demand to move the market price.
> **Detection**: Order-to-trade ratio > 100:1, cancellation rate > 95%, orders placed at prices far from the spread.
> **ZH:** 下大额订单但意图在执行前撤销，制造虚假供需假象以移动市场价格。检测：挂单成交比 > 100:1、撤单率 > 95%、挂单价格远离买卖价差。

**Layering 分层操纵**
> **EN:** Placing multiple orders at different price levels on one side of the order book to create an illusion of depth, then trading on the opposite side.
> **ZH:** 在订单簿一侧多个价位挂单制造深度假象，然后在另一侧交易。

**Pump and Dump 拉高出货**
> **EN:** Coordinated buying to inflate a token's price, followed by selling into the artificially high price. Often orchestrated through social media groups.
> **ZH:** 协同买入推高代币价格，然后在人为高价卖出。通常通过社交媒体群组策划。

**Insider Trading 内幕交易**
> **EN:** Trading on material non-public information, such as upcoming exchange listings or protocol announcements.
> **ZH:** 根据重大非公开信息进行交易，如即将上币或协议公告。

### 12.3 Bridge Security 跨链桥安全

**EN:** Cross-chain bridges are the single highest-value target in crypto. Over $2 billion was stolen from bridges in 2022 alone.

**Attack vectors:**
1. **Validator compromise**: If an attacker controls enough validators (e.g., 5/9 multisig in Ronin), they can approve fraudulent withdrawals.
2. **Smart contract bugs**: Signature verification errors (Wormhole), upgrade vulnerabilities.
3. **Message replay**: Cross-chain messages being replayed on the destination chain.
4. **Liquidity attacks**: Draining one side of the bridge's liquidity pool.

**Mitigation strategies:**
- Decentralize validator set with high threshold (e.g., >2/3)
- Independent security monitoring (Forta, Tenderly)
- Rate limiting on bridge transfers
- Separate upgrade keys from operational keys
- Circuit breakers that pause the bridge on anomaly detection

**ZH:** 跨链桥是加密世界最高价值目标。仅2022年就有超过20亿美元从桥被盗。攻击向量包括验证者被攻破、智能合约漏洞、消息重放和流动性攻击。缓解策略：去中心化验证者集合、独立安全监控、桥传输速率限制、升级密钥与操作密钥分离、异常检测熔断。

### 14.2 Key Regulatory Concepts 关键监管概念

**Security vs. Utility Token (Howey Test)**
> **EN:** The Howey Test (US Supreme Court, 1946) determines whether an asset is a security: (1) Investment of money, (2) in a common enterprise, (3) with expectation of profits, (4) derived from the efforts of others. Most ICO tokens arguably pass this test.

**Travel Rule (FATF Recommendation 16)**
> **EN:** Virtual Asset Service Providers (VASPs) must share originator and beneficiary information for transfers above a certain threshold. Implementation varies by jurisdiction but generally requires: sender name, sender account, recipient name, recipient account.

**MiCA (Markets in Crypto-Assets)**
> **EN:** The EU's comprehensive crypto framework covering: issuer obligations for asset-referenced tokens and e-money tokens, CASP authorization requirements, market abuse prevention, consumer protection, environmental disclosures.

**ZH:**
- 豪威测试：投资金钱 + 共同事业 + 期望利润 + 依赖他人努力 → 证券
- 旅行规则：VASP 必须共享转账双方信息
- MiCA：欧盟综合加密框架，覆盖发行方义务、CASP授权、市场滥用防范、消费者保护

## Chapter 15: Comparative Case Studies 对比案例研究

### 15.1 Traditional: Barings Bank Collapse (1995)
### 传统：巴林银行倒闭（1995年）

**EN:** Nick Leeson, a derivatives trader in Singapore, accumulated $1.4 billion in losses through unauthorized Nikkei futures trading. The bank, founded in 1762, collapsed.

**Root causes:**
- Leeson controlled both trading AND settlement — no segregation of duties
- Management ignored red flags from auditors
- Lack of understanding of derivatives risk at senior levels
- Inadequate oversight of remote offices

**Lesson:** Segregation of duties is non-negotiable. No single person should control front-to-back trading operations.

**ZH:** 尼克·里森在新加坡积累14亿美元未经授权的日经期货交易亏损。成立于1762年的银行倒闭。根因：里森同时控制交易和结算、管理层忽视审计红旗、高层不理解衍生品风险、远程办公室监管不足。教训：职责分离不可妥协。

### 15.2 Crypto: FTX Collapse (2022)
### 加密：FTX 崩盘（2022年）

**EN:** FTX, valued at $32 billion, collapsed within days after revelations of commingled customer funds and related-party lending to Alameda Research.

**Parallels with Barings:**
- Concentration of power (SBF had no board oversight)
- Commingling of customer and proprietary funds
- No independent risk function
- Auditors failed to detect massive fraud

**Unique crypto factors:**
- Offshore structure with no regulatory oversight
- FTT token used as collateral — circular dependency
- Social media-driven bank run accelerated the collapse

**ZH:** 估值320亿美元的FTX在数日内崩溃。与巴林银行相似之处：权力集中（无董事会监督）、客户与自有资金混用、无独立风控职能、审计师未能发现大规模欺诈。加密特有因素：离岸结构无监管、FTT代币做抵押品造成循环依赖、社交媒体驱动的挤兑加速崩溃。

### 15.3 Bridge: Comparing Traditional Banking Runs to Crypto Bank Runs
### 对比：传统银行挤兑 vs 加密银行挤兑

| Factor | Traditional Bank Run | Crypto Bank Run / DeFi Run |
|--------|---------------------|---------------------------|
| Speed | Days to weeks | Minutes to hours |
| Transparency | Opaque balance sheets | On-chain reserves visible in real-time |
| Circuit breakers | Banking holidays, withdrawal limits | Smart contract automation, no human override |
| Lender of last resort | Central bank support | No equivalent for most crypto entities |
| Contagion | Interbank lending network | Protocol composability (one protocol's failure cascades) |
